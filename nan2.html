Product Description

Important Notice: There is no substitutes of or equivalent to breast-milk Nestlé® NAN® OPTIPRO® 2 is an Follow-up Formula with OPTIPRO designed for infants after 6 months onwards as the liquid part of the diet during and after weaning, in addition to other foods. It is not to be used as a breast milk substitute during the first 6 months of life. Nestlé® NAN® OPTIPRO® 2 contains: - OPTIPRO®, an optimized protein blend exclusively to NAN® that is unique in both quality and amount. It supplies the adequate amount of proteins needed for your baby’s optimal growth without overloading immature organs. - BIFIDUS BL is a probiotic culture that helps to reinforce baby’s defense system. - DHA and ARA, two special fatty acids found in breast milk, which are important for your baby’s immune defense system and contribute to the development of brain and vision.
